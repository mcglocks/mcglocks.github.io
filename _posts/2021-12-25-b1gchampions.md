Let me set the stage. It is Saturday, December 4th, the first weekend in Ann Arbor after our victorious win over "the" Ohio State Buckeyes. That win was enjoyed by me in the comfort of Village Station in Mamaroneck. Back in Ann Arbor, it was to be a special weekend, enjoyed by my parents, Hugh and Mary, and the Michigan Pops Orchestra. It was our first concert in over two years!

I'll skip over the details of my day in one sentence. The mid-day was filled by a Pops rehearsal, and then a wonderful early dinner with the McCreadies at Gandy Dancer.

Back at 1361, Biggie (seen above) and I (seen below) changed into our Michigan attire and got ready to watch our team in their FIRST B1G Championship since the game's inception. As much as you may have expected this post to be about the game, the game was not very competitive. 42-3 was the score in favor of The Wolverines as we took care of business in the exact manner that was expected of us; we were the dominant team and it showed. Now that the game has concluded, I can get to the fun stuff!

![image](https://maxliving847609587.wordpress.com/wp-content/uploads/2021/12/screen-shot-2021-12-24-at-9.51.29-pm.png?w=292)

As soon as the game concluded, we rushed South U. It seemed as if others had the same idea! Michigan staples such as Mr. Brightside, Pump It Up, and (It's Great to be a) Michigan Wolverine were chanted as the crowd jumped and cheered!

The police had barricaded the road to prevent cars from coming, and more people continued to pour into the intersection with Church St.

![image](https://maxliving847609587.wordpress.com/wp-content/uploads/2021/12/img_0807-1.jpeg?w=1024)

Here you can see Drew, Alec and I posed for a picture amidst the sea of students! It was an overall excellent time. We headed back home to pick up a few things, but rushed back when Dad called to alert us of the basketball team's presence.

An inebriated Brandon Johns handed him his phone, but according to my dad he took a "blurry photo, maybe a video." Very funny. We parted ways with Mom and Dad and headed home to wrap up an incredible evening in preparation for the next day's concert!
